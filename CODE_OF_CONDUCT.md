# Rift Code of Conduct

Welcome!

Rift is an open project built by people from different backgrounds and experience levels. We want this community to be respectful, inclusive, and safe for everyone.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that helps create a positive environment:

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions.
- Assume good intent and communicate with clarity.
- Accept feedback gracefully and focus on solutions.
- Be patient with newcomers and helpful when possible.
- Respect different opinions, experiences, and communication styles.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior is not acceptable in this community:

- Harassment, hate speech, personal attacks, or discrimination.
- Trolling, insulting, or deliberately disruptive behavior.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Sexual language or imagery in non-appropriate contexts.
- Repeatedly ignoring community boundaries or moderator decisions.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces, including:

- GitHub issues, pull requests, and discussions.
- Project communication channels.
- Any public spaces where someone is representing the Rift project.

---

## Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately:

- Email: `wdkq@bladerunn.in`

Please include as much detail as possible (what happened, where, when, and who was involved). All reports will be reviewed seriously and handled confidentially whenever possible.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct. They may take any action they consider appropriate, including warning, temporary restrictions, or removal from project spaces.

Decisions are made to protect the community and keep collaboration productive.
